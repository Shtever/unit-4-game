Turtles in Turmoil

You will be given a Target Number at the start of the game.
Click on a turtle to add points to your total score.
Each turtle has a random value between 1 and 12.
Win the game by matching your total score to the Target Number.
You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
Each time the game starts, the values of the turtles will be changed.

If you're feeling lucky, click on Shredder for a sudden death match!
