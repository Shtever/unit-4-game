Crystal Collector

You will be given a random Target Number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. Each crystal has a random value between 1 and 12.

You win the game by matching your total score to the Target Number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

Each time the game starts, the values of the crystals will be changed.
